Our Story

A tradition of taste in the heart of Kurnool

What started as a small family kitchen has grown into Kurnool's beloved bakery. Every morning before dawn, our bakers begin crafting the breads, cakes, and sweets that our community has come to love.

We source the finest ingredients — real butter, farm-fresh eggs, pure ghee, and seasonal fruits — because we believe great baking begins with honest materials. From our classic Andhra sweets and tangy pickles to our rich chocolate cakes, every item is made with the same care you'd put into feeding your own family.
